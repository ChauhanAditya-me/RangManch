About Us

RangManch - Zone of Creativity & Vision

This club provides an excellent platform for students who are passionate about filmmaking, storytelling, and visual arts. Our Film Club at VIT Pune is an interdisciplinary club that welcomes anyone interested in directing, cinematography, screenwriting, editing, and sound design. The club offers numerous opportunities to learn more about film production, creative storytelling, and technical skills in filmmaking throughout the year.

We firmly believe in teamwork and collaboration. Together, we aim to foster technical expertise and creative expression in the art of filmmaking. From producing short films and documentaries to organizing workshops and film screenings, we encourage and nurture enthusiastic students. At the Film Club, we create a supportive environment that combines learning, creativity, and community. We are a passionate team of storytellers!
