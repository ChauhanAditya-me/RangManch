About Us

This club provides an excellent platform for students who want to explore their interests in the field of FILMMAKING and MEDIA PRODUCTION. The filmmaking club at VIT Pune is an interdisciplinary club that welcomes all those passionate about storytelling, cinematography, editing, direction, and production. Our club offers countless opportunities to learn more about film theory, camera work, editing software, scriptwriting, and film direction throughout the year.
